---

# Workflow

1. list candidate stories
2. identify dependencies and blockers
3. size complexity as S, M, or L
4. identify which specialists are needed
5. recommend sequence
6. separate committed scope from stretch scope

## Output

- sprint goal
- committed items
- stretch items
- blockers
- sequencing
- risks
